Mpiftekia

DESCRIPTION

This is my favourite recipe for chicken burgers aka kotompiftekia. Creds to my partner for the recipe.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1kg minced chicken
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup grated bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup oats (optional: blended)
  • 1 large onion, finely cut
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cinnamon
  • paprika
  • minced garlic

STEPS

  1. Add the grated vegetables in a food processor and blend them into a pulp.
  2. Mix the oats and the mince, in a large bowl.
  3. Add the vegetables pulp into the oats mince.
  4. Mix everything together and add the seasonings.
  5. Mix and form burger patties.
  6. Add to a pan covered in non-stick cooking paper and cook for approx 40-45 mins at 200 degrees.
